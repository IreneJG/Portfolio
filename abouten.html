Hey

I am a Creative problem solver with Product Design skills in both the Physical and Digital environment.

I started solving problems, with solutions in the form of industrial product and as the years passed with more inclination towards the handmade product. I realized that what I was designing was based on providing a specific solution, without taking into account that it would not last in time, and that it was stuck in a linear economy model. I decided to specialize in sustainable design, and thus update my way of "solving": more conscious and more respectful of the environment, people and the future. I understood that sometimes the solution to promote sustainable consumption is not a physical product, but a service supported by technology, and I entered the world of digital products.
