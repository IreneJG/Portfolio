Hello! my name is ...
Irene Jimenez Garcia

As you may have noticed, I'm a designer. I was born on the coast of the Basque Country, and grew up with one foot on each side of the border: between French and Spanish culture. I have lived and studied in France throughout my youth and now I have settled in Barcelona for its charm and opportunities. Apart from my passion and tireless strength for "CREATING", I am also a lover of the outdoors, travelling, art and above all... finding solutions.

●

My background ______________________________________________

I studied Industrial and Craft Product Design, and specialized in Sustainable Product Design. That means having a conscious and responsible approach to the environment, people and the future way of design. Recently, the interest and curiosity for the Digital world and Service Design, took me to make a specialization course of UX & UI Design, and now I practice in both specialties
